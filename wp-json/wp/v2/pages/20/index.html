We are a group of translators who translate games into Polish in our free time. If you’d like us to help you too, or if you’d like to join us, please email us or message us on our Discord server.<\/strong><\/p>\n

Our main goal is to make it possible for others who don’t speak English as well as we do, to play the games they love with the ability to understand everything that they see. <\/strong><\/p>\n

When we work with developers we try to make sure that both sides work together under the best conditions, and we are open to any suggestions about translations.<\/strong><\/p>\n

In the tabs above you can see what games we have translated so far and also the people from our group who have helped us translate them.<\/strong><\/p>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n

Why us ?<\/span><\/p>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n

We are not a normal translation team, we do it in our free time and completely for free.<\/strong><\/p>\n

In our group we make sure that there are people who are fluent in English and Polish, so we can ensure that the translations will be accurate and perfect.<\/strong><\/p>\n

We can translate from English to Polish as well as the other way around, making sure that the translations make as much sense as possible no matter how hard it may be.<\/strong><\/p>\n

If you are looking for a team of translators who not only translate accurately and accurately but also do it with passion and commitment, choose us and we will help you with exactly that.<\/strong><\/p>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/section>\n
